'GeGuide' is an application design aimed at people in precarious circumstances and living in Geneva, Switzerland.

GEGUIDE | 2023

UI/UX
User Tests
Figma
Design

You will find a map with all the main associations and places offering help and urgent care for people in need. It was designed to be simple and accessible at any time and place. The target audience consists of people living in precarious situations as well as social workers from Geneva. Nowadays, most people have smartphones, even homeless people. There is a real need for an app like 'GeGuide' that could be accessible anywhere, even without wifi.
